WHAT DO I OFFER?

My focus is to provide your business with a professional website for an affordable price with no hassle to you. I create and maintain the site, adding any updates or changes that you need as well as hosting the website and the domain name. I will create you a website that runs and looks great with a focus on user inferfaces and user experience.


User Experience

This is my #1 priority for every site I create! I make the important information that your customers easy to access, and always focus on user experience and user interface.

Mobile

Over half of all web traffic comes from mobile devices, so it is essential that the site is adaptable for them. I ensure that any user on your site from a mobile browser has just as good of an experience as someone from a computer.

Hosting

As well as the creation of the website, my service includes the hosting which includes hosting the domain and the website on the internet, so that is accessible by your customers.

Support

During and after website creation I am always available by email to help make any changes you need or to answer any questions you have.



THE PROCESS

  1. Fill out the contact form or send me an email to get in contact.

  2. You will be sent a form to fill out with your business information, any pictures, any requests you have, the tabs you want, and other questions about what you want on the site.

  3. A prototype for your website and will be created and I will request any additional information I need for the finished product.

  4. You will be shown the finished product, and I will make any changes that you request until you are ready for the site to go public.

  5. After we find a domain name for your website I will publish it online and at this point it is finished and available to your customers! I am always available to make changes to your site, just send an email with your request and after it is finished I will send you a confirmation email back.


If you have any additional questions about this process please ask! I make it my mission to make building your business's website as easy as possible.
